FAQ

Common questions

Can the AI give price estimates?

Yes. We train it with your pricing ranges. "An oil change starts at $39.99" or "Brake pads typically run $150-$250 depending on the vehicle." It gives ballparks without committing you to a price before diagnosis.

What if someone has an emergency or tow situation?

The AI recognizes urgency and can immediately text your manager or forward the call. For after-hours emergencies, it collects details and sends you an urgent alert.

Does it integrate with my shop management software?

We work with most major shop management systems. During setup, we'll configure integrations with your existing tools, or it works standalone if you prefer to keep it simple.

What about the service reminder texts? Won't customers find them annoying?

Customers opt in, and they can reply STOP anytime. In practice, most people appreciate the reminder. It's like a dentist appointment reminder but for their car. It drives repeat business.

How quickly can we get started?

Most shops are live within 48 hours. We handle all the setup. You just review and approve.

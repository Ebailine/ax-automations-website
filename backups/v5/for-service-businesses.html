Frequently asked questions

How long does setup take?
Most businesses are fully live in under five minutes. You connect your Google Business Profile, choose your preferences, and Bynde starts working immediately. No technical skills required.
Will the AI responses sound like me?
Yes. During onboarding, Bynde learns your business voice and tone. Every review response and phone interaction reflects your brand, not a generic template. You can review and adjust the tone at any time.
What happens when a caller needs a real person?
If the AI cannot handle a request or the caller asks for a human, it immediately routes the call to your designated staff member and sends you a transcript of the conversation so far.
Can I use Bynde with my existing tools?
Absolutely. Bynde integrates with Google Business Profile, Square, Stripe, PayPal, and most popular scheduling platforms. It works alongside your current workflow, not instead of it.
Is there a contract or commitment?
No long term contracts. Bynde offers quarterly and annual billing. You can cancel anytime and your data stays yours.

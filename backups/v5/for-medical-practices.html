Frequently asked questions

Is Bynde HIPAA compliant?
Bynde is designed with HIPAA awareness in mind. AI review responses never include protected health information. Phone transcripts are stored securely and access is limited to authorized practice staff.
Will AI review responses sound professional enough for a medical practice?
Absolutely. Bynde is trained to respond in a clinical yet warm tone appropriate for healthcare. Responses never disclose patient details and always maintain the professionalism your patients expect.
How does the AI phone handle urgent medical calls?
You configure escalation rules during setup. If a caller reports an emergency or urgent symptom, the AI immediately routes the call to your on call staff or directs them to call 911. Non urgent calls are handled normally.
Do I need to replace my existing practice management software?
No. Bynde is not a practice management system. It works alongside your existing PMS, EHR, and scheduling tools. Bynde focuses specifically on Google reviews and phone answering.
How quickly can my practice go live?
Most practices are fully operational within 10 minutes. Connect your Google Business Profile, set your preferences, and Bynde starts working. No contracts with your IT department required.

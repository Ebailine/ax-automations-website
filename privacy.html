Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Bynde ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by Bynde, provides an automated business communications platform including review management, appointment reminders, SMS communications, and business analytics. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform and services.

By using Bynde, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Business Account Information

When you sign up for Bynde, we collect:

  • Business name, address, phone number, and email
  • Google Business Profile connection details
  • Account owner name and contact information
  • Billing and payment information

2.2 Customer Data (Provided by Your Business)

To provide our services, your business may upload or sync the following customer data:

  • Customer names, phone numbers, and email addresses
  • Review data (content, ratings, timestamps)
  • Call logs and communication history
  • Transaction and appointment data

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device and browser information
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Usage data and interaction logs within the platform

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and operate the Bynde platform, including automated review management, appointment reminders, and SMS communications
  • Generate AI-powered review responses and business analytics
  • Send review requests and customer communications on behalf of your business
  • Process billing and manage your account
  • Improve our services and develop new features
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. SMS & Messaging Communications

Bynde sends SMS and text messages on behalf of businesses to their customers for review requests, appointment reminders, and customer follow-ups.

  • Message frequency varies based on business activity and customer interactions
  • Message and data rates may apply — standard messaging and data rates from your carrier apply
  • Opt-out: Reply STOP to any message to opt out of future communications
  • Help: Reply HELP to any message or email hello@bynde.io for support

TCPA Compliance: We comply with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) and all applicable federal and state regulations regarding SMS and telephone communications. Messages are sent only to individuals whose business has represented that proper consent has been obtained. Businesses using Bynde are responsible for obtaining and maintaining valid consent from their customers before adding them to the platform for SMS communications. We maintain records of consent and provide clear opt-out mechanisms in every message.

5. Data Ownership

Customer data belongs to the business, not Bynde. You retain full ownership of all customer data you provide to or generate through our platform. We act as a data processor on your behalf. If you terminate your account, you may request a full export of your data before deletion.

6. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our platform:

  • Google Business Profile API — to manage and respond to reviews
  • Twilio — to send and receive SMS messages
  • OpenAI — to power AI-generated review responses and other AI features
  • Stripe — to process payments and manage billing
  • Supabase — for secure data storage and management

Each third-party provider has their own privacy policy governing their use of data. We only share the minimum data necessary for each service to function.

7. Data Sharing & Selling

We do NOT sell your data to third parties. We do not share, rent, or trade personal information or customer data with third parties for their marketing purposes.

Data is NOT used for marketing purposes beyond the business's own customer communications managed through our platform.

We may disclose information only when:

  • Required by law, subpoena, or legal process
  • Necessary to protect our rights, safety, or property
  • With your explicit consent
  • To our service providers who assist in operating the platform (as listed in Section 6), under strict confidentiality agreements

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, access controls, and regular security reviews. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide our services. Upon account termination:

  • You may request a data export within 30 days of cancellation
  • Customer data will be permanently deleted within 90 days of account termination
  • We may retain anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes
  • Certain data may be retained as required by law (e.g., billing records)

To request data deletion at any time, contact us at hello@bynde.io.

10. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Data portability

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@bynde.io.

11. Children's Privacy

Bynde is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of Bynde after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: hello@bynde.io

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Bynde ("we", "us", "our") operates the Bynde platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Business name, type, phone number, and email address
  • Google Business Profile data (reviews, ratings) when you connect your account
  • Customer names and phone numbers that you add to the platform
  • Usage data and analytics about how you interact with the dashboard

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide AI-generated review responses for your business
  • To send SMS review requests to your customers on your behalf
  • To provide missed call text-back services
  • To generate analytics and reputation reports
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To improve our services and develop new features

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share information only with:

  • Twilio — to send SMS messages and handle phone calls
  • OpenAI — to generate AI review responses (review text only, no personal data)
  • Stripe — to process payments
  • Google — to sync your Business Profile reviews (with your authorization)
  • Supabase — to store your data securely

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (SSL/TLS), secure database storage with row-level security, and never store plaintext passwords.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will remove your data within 30 days, except where required by law.

6. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access and export your data at any time from your dashboard
  • Update or correct your information in Settings
  • Delete your account by contacting us
  • Opt out of marketing communications

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.

Common questions

Is there a contract or minimum commitment?
We offer monthly and annual billing. You can cancel at the end of your billing period with no penalties. Annual plans save ~15%. There are no setup fees on any plan.
Are there any setup fees?
No. There are no setup fees on any Bynde plan. Guided onboarding, Google Business Profile connection, AI phone configuration, and industry template setup are all included at no extra cost.
What happens if I go over my phone minutes?
Overage minutes are billed at $0.25/minute. You'll receive alerts when you're approaching your limit so there are no surprises. You can also upgrade your plan at any time to get more included minutes.
Will callers know they're talking to AI?
Most cannot tell. The AI is trained on natural conversation using your business details, FAQs, and services. It doesn't sound robotic or scripted. If a caller needs a real person, it transfers the call or takes a detailed message.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes. You can upgrade at any time and the price difference is prorated. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
How does the AI phone receptionist work?
We provision a local phone number for your business (or port your existing number). Calls are answered 24/7 by our AI, which can take messages, answer FAQs from your knowledge base, book appointments on your calendar, and forward urgent calls to you directly.
How is Bynde different from Birdeye or Podium?
Bynde includes an AI phone receptionist, smart scheduling, forms and waivers, and automation flows — features that competitors charge $350-$500/mo for separately. Our Pro plan at $199/mo covers what would cost $800+/mo pieced together from other vendors. Plus, we have no setup fees and offer flexible monthly or annual billing.
What industries does Bynde support?
Bynde has pre-built templates for 10 industries including dental, medical, salon/spa, legal, home services, automotive, fitness, restaurants, real estate, and veterinary. Templates auto-configure your AI receptionist script, review request messaging, and forms for your specific industry.
I'm switching from Podium/Broadly. Is migration free?
100% free. We handle the entire transition so there's no gap in your coverage. Most migrations complete in under 48 hours.

Common questions

How long does setup take?▾

Under 5 minutes. Connect your Google Business Profile, set your preferences, and you're live. Most people finish during their first coffee break.

Will the AI sound robotic?▾

No. It learns your tone and writes like you would. Customers won't know the difference. You can review and edit any response before it posts.

What if I want to approve responses before they post?▾

You can. Choose auto post for hands off management, or review first if you want to approve each response. Switch anytime.

Do I need to be technical?▾

Not at all. If you can use email, you can use Ax. No coding, no complicated setup. We handle the technical side.

Can I try it before committing?▾

Book a demo and we will show you everything live with your actual business data. See it working before you pay anything.

What happens if I cancel?▾

Your data stays yours. Export anytime. No penalties, no hidden fees. We believe you should stay because the product works, not because you're locked in.

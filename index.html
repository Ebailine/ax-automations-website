Platform

Everything your business needs. One platform.

Phone receptionist, review management, scheduling, forms, waivers, and smart automation flows. All in one place.

AI Phone Receptionist

Answers every call 24/7. Takes messages, books appointments, and handles FAQs with a custom voice and script built for your industry. Includes call forwarding, voicemail mode, minute tracking, and usage alerts.

Smart Review Management

Sends review requests after every service automatically. Writes and posts on brand responses. Follows up at 3 and 7 days. Turns reviews into social content and tracks what your competitors are doing.

Appointment Scheduling

Built in calendar and online booking that syncs with your phone receptionist. Sends confirmation and reminder emails, recovers no shows automatically, and nudges customers to rebook.

Forms and Waivers

Digital intake forms, liability waivers, and consent forms with e signatures. Sent automatically after booking so customers arrive ready. Includes a customer portal for easy access.

Smart Automation Flows

No show recovery, win back campaigns, welcome sequences, post service upsells, birthday rewards, seasonal promotions, payment follow ups, and staff performance tracking. Set them up once and let them run.

60+ Industry Templates

Pick your industry on signup and everything configures itself. Phone scripts, review messaging, automation flows, and forms are all tailored to how your business actually works.

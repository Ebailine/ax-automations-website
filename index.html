About

Built by Ethan, a Drexel student who got tired of watching local businesses lose money to voicemail.

I'm 21, studying finance at Drexel University in Philadelphia. I started Ax Automations after watching my friends' parents lose customers because they couldn't answer the phone while doing the actual work.

A plumber can't pick up mid-job. A dentist can't pause a filling. But their customers don't wait. They call the next place on Google.

So I built a system using AI tools that already exist (OpenAI, Twilio, Google) and packaged them so small businesses don't need to be tech-savvy to use them. I handle the setup, the tech, and the monitoring. You just run your business.

We're new. I'm not going to pretend we've been around for 10 years. But the technology isn't new, and I'm betting my reputation on every client we take on. No contracts. If it doesn't work, you stop paying.

FAQ

Questions we get a lot.

Will my customers know they're talking to AI?

Most can't tell. Our AI is trained on natural conversation. It doesn't sound robotic or scripted. And if a caller needs a real human, the AI hands off seamlessly. You get a notification, the caller gets helped.

How long does setup take?

Most clients are live within 48 hours. We handle everything. You just need to answer a few questions about your business and approve the final setup.

Is there a contract?

No long-term contracts. Month-to-month. If we're not making you money, you shouldn't be paying us.

What kind of businesses do you work with?

Mostly service businesses: plumbers, HVAC, dentists, lawyers, contractors, salons, auto shops. If your business gets phone calls and needs reviews, we can help.

What if I already have a receptionist?

Great. We're not replacing them. We handle the calls they can't: after hours, weekends, when they're on another line. Think of us as backup that never calls in sick.

How do I see results?

You get a dashboard showing every call answered, text sent, review requested, and lead captured. Real numbers, updated in real time.

Want to see it work for your business?

15 minutes. I'll show you how the system works and you can decide if it makes sense. No pressure.

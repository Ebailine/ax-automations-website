Why Ax exists

Small business owners are the backbone of every neighborhood. They pour everything into their craft, whether that is cutting hair, fixing teeth, or keeping homes spotless. But the administrative side of running a business keeps growing, and it pulls them away from the work they love.

Ax was built to fix that. We take the repetitive, time consuming tasks that drain your energy and automate them with AI that actually sounds like you. Reviews get collected and responded to. Calls get answered and booked. Your dashboard shows the full picture without five different logins.

We are a small team based in Philadelphia, obsessed with building tools that let business owners get back to doing great work.

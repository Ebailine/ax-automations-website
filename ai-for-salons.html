FAQ

Common questions

Can clients book with a specific stylist?

Yes. The AI knows each stylist's schedule, specialties, and availability. It books the right person every time.

What if someone wants a service I don't offer?

The AI is trained on your exact service menu. If someone asks for something you don't offer, it suggests the closest alternative or takes a message.

Does it handle cancellations and reschedules?

Yes. Clients can cancel or reschedule via text or phone, and the AI automatically opens up the slot and can text your waitlist to fill it.

Can it send appointment reminders?

Absolutely. Automated reminders go out 48 hours and 2 hours before each appointment, reducing no-shows by up to 35%.

What about walk-ins vs. appointments?

The AI can let callers know about walk-in availability or encourage booking. You set the rules based on your salon's preference.

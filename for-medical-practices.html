Common questions

Is this HIPAA compliant?▾

Ax never accesses, stores, or transmits protected health information (PHI). We only manage your public Google reviews and handle appointment scheduling calls. No patient records are involved.

Will the AI mention specific treatments in review responses?▾

Never. The AI is trained to respond professionally without referencing specific treatments, diagnoses, or any details that could be considered protected health information.

How does the phone AI handle urgent calls?▾

You configure what counts as urgent. The AI can route emergency calls directly to your on call staff, take messages for callbacks, or direct callers to emergency services when appropriate.

Can I approve review responses before they post?▾

Absolutely. Many practices prefer to review responses first, especially for negative reviews. You can set auto post for positive reviews and manual review for anything below 4 stars.

How long does setup take?▾

Under 5 minutes. Your office manager can set it up between patients. Connect Google Business, set your preferences, and you're live.

Can I try it before committing?▾

Book a demo and we will show you everything live with your actual practice data. See it working before you pay anything.
